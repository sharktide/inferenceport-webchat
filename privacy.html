Your Data, In Context

InferencePort needs some information to run chats, store media, synchronize signed-in settings, enforce limits, and keep the service safe. This policy explains what is collected, why it is used, and where third-party services may be involved.

What We Collect

  • Connection and security data such as IP address, user agent, timestamps, and verification state.
  • Authentication and account identifiers when you sign in.
  • Chat messages, uploaded files, saved memories, personalization prompts, and generated media when you use those features.
  • Guest identifiers and client identifiers stored locally so guest sessions and per-device limits work correctly.
  • Usage, subscription, and quota data returned by service providers that handle billing and rate limits.

Short Version

The website does not operate without processing data. It stores the information needed to provide chats, uploads, memories, settings, moderation and abuse prevention, and account-linked features.

How Data Is Used

Core product delivery Chat history, prompt settings, and media files are used so the interface can reopen previous work and keep your sessions coherent.
Security and reliability Verification, device session tracking, and operational logging help reduce abuse, fraud, and accidental account conflicts.
Billing and limits Subscription and usage systems are queried so the website can show plan data, tool quotas, and free-tier limits like daily web searches.

Third-Party Services

  • Authentication and account services may process sign-in requests and account metadata.
  • Verification providers may process challenge tokens and connection metadata.
  • Model, search, generation, billing, and quota services may receive the data necessary to complete your request.

Avoid submitting highly sensitive information unless you understand that third-party infrastructure may be involved in fulfilling the request.

Retention And Control

  • You can delete chats, media, memories, or accounts using the website controls that are available to you.
  • Operational logs, short-lived backups, or abuse-prevention records may persist temporarily where needed for reliability or security.
  • Guest content can still be stored for the duration needed to support the guest experience and cleanup policy.

Security Note

InferencePort uses protective controls, but no internet service can promise absolute security. Review generated outputs before relying on them, and do not assume uploaded or generated content is risk-free.

This privacy policy is a product-level explanation of how the current website works and may evolve as features or providers change.
